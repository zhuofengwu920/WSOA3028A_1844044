Origami

Author: Kloma Yellow
h6>Created: 19 April 2020
Last updated: 26 June 2020

Origami, basically making something with some paper by folding it, often without using glue or other material to attach paper to other paper.

This played a huge part in my childhood, in my family there was not a lot of free money. The parents didn’t earn a lot, there are little to no toys around or at less no new ones. As the eldest one amongst my siblings knowing origami or making paper toys as we say then helped a lot. As I made more and got more advance with it the more I like it. Perhaps is the ability to turn the newspaper into something that everyone wanted brings my young self a sense of accomplishment.

However, the place that I learn this may be surprising. I discovered a book that had a guide to making these paper toys, I remember it was a manga don’t remember what story it had but the last few pages are always a guide to make an origami something. Could a hat, sometime they can provide a longer and more complex guide on a more difficult origami. I used to try for hours to make one of these, and the result is often a failure. It could be that I didn’t understand the steps properly, but because the next step builds on that so naturally it didn’t work if I didn’t understand just 1 step. But more often the paper broke in steps, back then I thought I was just doing it wrong. Only now I realise that newspapers are not the paper for origami.

I was forced to stop this since I came to Joburg, the suitable paper are just hard to find here. I have been in countless art store and supplies here, and papers like tissue foil one of the more common origami paper are not to be found. This limited me in advancing and attempt more difficult origami. I still make some simple ones now and then but without challenging myself I find it difficult to be motivated to make some of these.

It is frustrating to have your progress in something stop due to outside factors and not your commitment. It is like the world is trying to stop you for no good reason.
